Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on project scope, company size, and timeline. Most sustainability audits range from $5,000-$15,000, while ongoing implementation support is typically structured as monthly retainers. We provide detailed proposals after an initial consultation.

How long does a typical engagement last?

Initial audits and strategy development usually take 4-8 weeks. Implementation support can range from 3 months to 2+ years depending on your goals. Many clients start with an audit, then continue with ongoing support.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! We work with organizations of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. We offer scaled solutions appropriate for different budgets and organizational complexities.

What industries do you specialize in?

Our team has experience across manufacturing, technology, retail, hospitality, healthcare, and professional services. We adapt our methodologies to fit industry-specific challenges and regulations.

Can you help us achieve B Corp certification?

Yes! We've guided over 30 companies through B Corp certification. We conduct gap analyses, help improve your B Impact Assessment score, and support you through the verification process.
